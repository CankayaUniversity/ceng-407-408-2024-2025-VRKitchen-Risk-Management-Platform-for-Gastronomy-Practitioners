
This is a low polygon model of stylized burger with hand painted textures.

Available in three different formats.

Blender.blend, FBX and OBJ.

Clean topology.

Software used: Blender 4.3 and Krita

Texture  2048x2048 PNG - Diffuse Map only

Polygon Count: 

Faces - 1148	Vertices - 1009	    Triangles - 1953
 
Rendered in Blender Cycles

HDRI image was used for the rendered images which is not included in the package.
